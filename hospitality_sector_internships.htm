HOSPITALITY SECTOR INTERNSHIPS
     FOR TOURISM STUDENTS
 

The objective of our tourism sector internships is to develop your abilities and consolidate your knowledge of your major through the carrying out of a position at a hotel or tourism related company.

The internships can last from 3 to 6 months, during which time you will work 8 hours a day, 6 days a week. It is available year – round.

You will begin with a training period and later move on to occupy your assigned post, under which you will receive the aid and supervision of the hotel directors.

At the end of your internship, an integral evaluation will be done for each intern, and you will receive a corresponding document or certificate.

You can carry out your internship in the following areas:
• Reception
• Housekeeping (floor supervisor)
• Public Relations
• Organization of groups and conventions
• Food and beverage
• Kitchen



REQUIREMENTS:
• Excellent service attitude
• English communication at 80% (except for kitchen internships)
• Up to date card of health (for food & beverage and kitchen interns)
• Accident and health insurance valid in Cancun, Mexico

YOUR STAY IN CANCUN:
“CIFET” interns, (whether through us or the hotel) receive the following services:
• Work permit from the immigration authority (for foreigners)
• Reception at the airport and transportation to place of residence
• Introductory course to the internship program
• Supervision and technical aid throughout the internship
• Final evaluation and certificate for each intern
• Uniforms (loaned)
• Lodging in student residence or at employees lodging at the hotel
• Weekly change of linens and room cleaning
• One meal per day
• Daily transportation to and from internship



NOTES:
• If the lodging is outside the hotel where the internship is performed, the location will depend on the distance of the hotel from downtown Cancun as well as availability. In any case, we offer the security, cleanliness and comfort required for your stay.
• If the lodging is at the hotel where the internship is performed, it will be in houses, apartments or rooms designated for employee lodging.
• The “student lodging” is located in downtown Cancun and has private security.
• The houses and apartments are equipped with individual beds and lockers, refrigerator, microwave, water heater, iron, ironing board, and ceiling fans.
• Daily transportation to and from the hotel is offered by the hotel’s employee transportation, or you will receive vouchers for the use of local transportation.



MONTHLY INVESTMENT: $360 US dollars, tax included (For a double accommodation)
This investment includes the internship, as well as all the previously mentioned services.

PROCEDURE:
• Internship contract between CIFET and your university
• Internship request from your university
