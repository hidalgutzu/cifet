PROFESSIONAL TRAINING GETAWAY
    FOR TOURISM STUDENTS
 
The opportunity to learn the “know-how” behind one of the most important tourist destinations of Mexico and the Caribbean.



LEARNING..... AN UNFORGETTABLE EXPERIENCE
CIFET Cancun offer tourism majors the opportunity to receive professional training in the Mexican Caribbean, a successful and internationally recognized tourist destination, equipped with the infrastructure necessary to develop a high-profile and high-quality professional activity.

With more that 60,000 hotel rooms belonging to the most important hotel chains in the world and an internationally competitive complementary service infrastructure, the Mexican Caribbean is the most important destination in Mexico and the Caribbean. It receives more than 5 million tourists a year from various continents.


PROGRAM OBJECTIVES AND CHARACTERISTICS

The objective of this trip is to offer an integral and modern vision of the technology and criteria for the commercialization and operation of the principal activities involved in tourism and hotel management and to combine conferences and presentations from professionally recognized instructors with interactive visits, all while experiencing exchanges and direct participation in tours and recreational activities.

Each activity starts with an “in situ” explanation about the organization, commercialization, and operation. Following this, the trainees will participate directly in the activity so that the information received can be utilized and also to exchange observations with the organizers of each event.

At the beginning of the program, each participant will receive a folder containing the objectives of the program along with a list of the fundamental aspects which they should observe and evaluate in each activity. This will form the basis for any report which may have to be turned in according to the requirements of each university.

During their stay in Cancun, there will be free time allowing the participants to visit local attractions and observe the diversity, marketing, and animation of the destination, all of which will enrich their vision and projection as future professionals.

The trip can be specifically oriented toward any of the areas related to hotels and tourism.
