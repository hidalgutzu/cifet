ACCOMMODATION IN CANCUN  
 


During your stay in Cancun you will receive the following services (services vary according to the selected package)


• Pick up at the airport and transfer to the student residential complex

• Accommodation in student residential complex

• Accommodation in student residential complex in Cancun city (24 hour private security)

• Located in central area of the city, next to “Puerto Cancun”, the most important residential tourism development in Cancun, 3 blocks away from Av. Bonampak

• New houses, equipped with boiler, fridge, stove, microwave, cable TV and a ceiling fan

• Fully furnished with living room set and dining room set

• Air conditioning in each bedroom

•Accommodation includes weekly cleaning, change of bedding linen & towels, TV with cable, internet access, electricity and running (tap) water



• Food

• 3 meals a day: breakfast, lunch and dinner (depending of the package)

• Houses will also have a fridge, microwave and stove for students to prepare their own meals if desired



• Transportation

• Daily transportation Residential Complex – University – Residential Complex.

• Internships: Pass to be used in hotel transportation.

• Personalized service

• Each program will have an assigned coordinator whose sole responsibility is to attend to  any needs/concerns that may arise during  the group’s stay.
