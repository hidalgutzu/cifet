SPANISH LANGUAGE   
 


Being fluent in Spanish can open many doors for you. Spanish is the third most popular language in the world and is a mother tongue of more than 350 millions of people in more than 20 countries. When it comes to business communication it is quickly gaining popularity.

The classes are held in the morning, from 9am to 1:30pm, a total of 22 hours per day. In the afternoon a combination of entertainment and learning activities will allow you to keep practicing while having fun. Being immersed in the Mexican culture is the fastest and most fun way to learn!!

Spanish Programs:

• Beginner: 8 weeks, 176 hours.
• Intermediate: 15 weeks, 330 hours.
• Advanced:  16 weeks, 352 hours.
• Spanish for professionals:  48 weeks,  1,056 hours.


More advance and targeted  training focused on developing the grammar and vocabulary required to study in an academic institution.

Program length can be tailored to your group’s needs
