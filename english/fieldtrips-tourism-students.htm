FIELD TRIPS FOR HOSPITALITY AND TOURISM STUDENTS  
 


The opportunity to learn the essentials of hospitality business from behind the scenes of one of the most important touristic  destination in the world

The objective of this trip is to offer the students an opportunity to learn about the sophisticated technology and operation practices of the touristic organizations and hotels in Cancun. The internship will also provide the participants access to professionally recognized instructors in the field combined with on-site interactive presentations, all while directly experiencing and participating in tours and recreational activities.

Each activity starts with an “in situ” introduction to the organization, marketing, commercialization and operation of their product or service. Then, trainees will participate directly in the activity (as tourists) so they have both perspective of a touristic product/service.

At the beginning of the program, each participant receives a folder containing the objectives of the program along with a list of the aspects that they should observe and evaluate in each activity.

During their stay in Cancun, program participants will have enough time to visit local attractions and observe all aspects of their operations, all of which will enrich their understanding of the industry as future hospitality professionals.
